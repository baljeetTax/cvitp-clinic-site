Who qualifies for this clinic?

This clinic follows the Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP) guidelines. You may qualify if you have a modest income and a simple tax situation.

Generally eligible if:

  • You have a modest income (for example, low or fixed income)
  • Your tax situation is simple (employment income, pension, benefits, etc.)
  • You do not have self-employment or business income
  • You do not have rental income
  • You have not declared bankruptcy
  • You are filing a personal income tax return (not for a business)

If you're not sure, that's okay

If you’re unsure whether you qualify, you can still start the intake form. We’ll review your information and let you know if this clinic is the right fit or if another service would be better.

What to bring to your appointment

Having the right documents ready makes your appointment smoother and helps us complete your return accurately.

Identification & personal information

  • Government-issued photo ID (if available)
  • Your Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • Your spouse/partner’s full name and date of birth (if applicable)
  • Information about your dependents (names, dates of birth)

Tax slips

  • T4 slips (employment income)
  • T4A, T4A(P), T4A(OAS), T4E, or other benefit slips
  • T5 slips (investment income), if applicable
  • Any other tax slips you received

Receipts & other documents

  • Rent or property tax information (if applicable)
  • Medical expense receipts (if applicable)
  • Charitable donation receipts
  • Child care expense receipts
  • Last year’s Notice of Assessment (if available)
  • Direct deposit information (void cheque or banking details)

If you’re missing something, bring what you have. We’ll do our best to help you move forward.

How the clinic works

We offer two ways to have your taxes done: drop-off and online via Zoom. Both options are free and handled by trained community volunteers.

Option 1: Drop-off

Drop off your documents

  1. Complete the intake form and choose “Drop-off” as your appointment type.
  2. Bring your documents to the clinic at your scheduled time.
  3. Our volunteers prepare your return after you leave.
  4. We contact you by email if we have questions or when your return is ready.

This option is helpful if you prefer not to stay for a full appointment or have limited time.

Option 2: Zoom

Meet with us online

  1. Complete the intake form and choose “Zoom” as your appointment type.
  2. At your appointment time, join the Zoom meeting using the link we send you.
  3. Share your documents securely and review your information with a volunteer.
  4. We complete your return and confirm details with you.

This option is helpful if you prefer to stay at home or have mobility or transportation challenges.

About this clinic

This clinic is run by community volunteers as part of the Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP). Our goal is to make tax filing less stressful and more accessible for people with modest incomes and simple tax situations.

We believe that everyone deserves to access the benefits and credits they are entitled to. By offering free, friendly tax help, we hope to reduce barriers and support our neighbours in feeling more confident and secure.

Volunteers receive training and follow CVITP guidelines. While we cannot handle every type of tax situation, we will always do our best to point you in the right direction if another service would be more appropriate.

Privacy & consent

Your privacy matters. Information you share with this clinic is collected only for the purpose of helping prepare your income tax return and managing your appointment.

  • Your information is handled by trained volunteers following CVITP guidelines.
  • We only collect the information we need to help prepare your return.
  • We do not share your information outside of the tax preparation process, except as required by law.
  • We will permanantly delete your information upon filing of your taxes and will not have access to them afterwards.

If you have questions or concerns about how your information is used, please contact us by email before submitting the intake form.
